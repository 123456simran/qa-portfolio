# Akal Shaye

## Project Overview

Akal Shaye is an e-commerce platform designed for purchasing Sikh religious and cultural products online. 
The platform allows users to browse and order various Sikh accessories such as turbans, baaz (turban accessories), 
kadda, fifty (turban underband), small kangas, and other related products. The platform provides a seamless shopping 
experience with product browsing, order management, and user-friendly navigation.
