# Phido Tracker (BLE)

## Project Overview

Phido Tracker is a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) tracking application designed to help users 
locate and manage their personal belongings. The app allows users to connect multiple Phido tracking devices, 
monitor their last known location, receive separation alerts when a device moves out of Bluetooth range, 
and trigger the tracker to locate misplaced items such as keys, wallets, or bags.

## Domain

IoT / Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)

## Platform

- Mobile App

## Project Type

Bluetooth Device Tracking Application

## Key Features

- Connect up to 5 Phido BLE devices
- Bluetooth pairing and device management
- Track the last known location of connected devices
- Separation alerts when a device moves out of Bluetooth range
- Ring the tracker to locate misplaced items
- View connected devices
- Edit and manage device details
- Customize device ringtone
- Do Not Disturb (DND) mode
- Push notifications for tracking events
- Device status monitoring
- User-friendly device management
