# Apni Ride (Taxi Booking)

## Project Overview

Apni Ride is a taxi booking platform that enables customers to book vehicles either for self-drive or with a professional driver. 
The platform consists of a Customer Mobile App, an Admin Panel, and a Vendor Panel, allowing efficient management of bookings, 
drivers, vendors, vehicles, pricing, and customer operations.

## Domain

Transportation / Taxi Booking
